- Phase 8 should finish the MVP rather than introduce new platform risk such as live PTY/xterm integration or real model backends.
- Existing persisted app settings are sufficient for a real MVP settings surface if they are exposed cleanly and saved through the current app-state boundary.
- Visual polish should focus on clarity, hierarchy, and consistency across the existing screens rather than a broad redesign.

5. Capture final phase-state updates for the roadmap.
